Falling scores over consecutive periods signal deterioration. Key columns: trans_month, credit_loss_prob, tot_struct_risk_score, cbr_score, positive_events, times_30_dpd.

• score drop > 50 pts in 3 months
• model score diverges from bureau score > 100 pts
• score in bottom decile
